ℹ️ About

The grid shows 256 inputs of an 8-bit S-box, partitioned into cosets of the known linear subspace LSx.

Each step picks two groups from different cosets, learns δx → δy, and uses the DDT to find & merge compatible pairs.

Highlighted points = largest clique. After 8 steps, the clique gives the affine approximation A with |{x : S(x)=A(x)}| = clique size.

Cosets are ordered by an affine complement basis: merging any pair automatically pairs all other cosets at the same grid offset. Click groups to do manual merges, or use Auto/Step.
